At K=1200, L6 = 2:
- Provides backup individual
- Reduces stochastic extinction risk
- Stabilizes the system

At K=600, L6 = 1:
- No redundancy

- L0: K/2 (300 → 600)

- L6: K/600 or K/300 (1 → 2)
