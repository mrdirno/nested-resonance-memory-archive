## MECHANISM

### Tri-Trophic Balance

```
Prey (P0):
  - Logistic growth (K=600)
  - Consumed by mesopredator
  - Equilibrium: 300 (50% K)

Mesopredator (P1):
  - Gains energy from prey
  - Consumed by top predator
  - Equilibrium: 30

Top Predator (P2):
  - Gains energy from mesopredator
  - No predation pressure
  - Equilibrium: 10
```

### Why No Destabilization?

1. **Type II functional response** at all levels prevents overconsumption
2. **Parameter choice** in viable zone for each interaction
3. **Energy flow** cascades properly through levels
4. **Population sizes** sufficient to buffer stochasticity
