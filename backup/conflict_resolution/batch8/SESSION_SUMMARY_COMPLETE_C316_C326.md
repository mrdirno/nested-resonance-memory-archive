## MAJOR DISCOVERIES

### 1. Productivity-Chain Scaling Law

**K×2 → chain+1 with preserved stability**

- K=600: 6-7 levels stable
- K=1200: 7-8 levels stable
- K=2400: 8-9 levels stable

Formula: Chain length ≈ log₂(K/100) + 4

### 2. Universal 85% Stability at N=1

Bifurcation rate is constant regardless of chain length:
- Seven-trophic at K=600: 85%
- Eight-trophic at K=1200: 85%

The scaling law preserves stability rate.

### 3. 95% Ceiling with Redundancy

Stability progression:
- N=1: 85%
- N=2: 95%
- N=3: 95%

Maximum achievable is 95%; residual 5% is system-wide risk.

### 4. Immediate Collapse Mechanism

All collapses occur at cycle 6-7:
- Top predator dies before reproduction
- Binary outcome (stable or instant collapse)
- Critical period is first ~10 cycles

### 5. N=2 Is Optimal

Best investment for stability:
- Large gain N=1→N=2 (+10%)
- No gain N=2→N=3 (+0%)
- Diminishing returns beyond N=2
