### 1. Universal 85% Stability at N=1

Both chain lengths show identical rate:

- Eight-trophic at K=1200: 85%

The scaling law preserves bifurcation probability.

### 2. Immediate Collapse Mechanism

All collapses occur at cycle 6-7:
- Top predator dies before reproduction
- Binary outcome (stable or instant collapse)
- Critical period is first ~10 cycles

### 3. Redundancy Effect (N=2)

Adding second individual at top:
- Stability: 85% → 95%
- Risk reduction: 3× (15% → 5%)
- Efficient stability investment

### 4. Correlated Failure

Observed risk higher than independent:
- Expected: 0.15² = 2.3%
- Observed: 5%
- Events partially correlated
