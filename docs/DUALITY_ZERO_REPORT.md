## Key Research Arcs & Principles Discovered

### 1. The Metabolic Cliff & Memory Maintenance (C2058-C2062)
*   **PRIN-REPLENISHMENT-LOAD-LIMIT:** Simple energy replenishment, while beneficial, fights a losing thermodynamic battle at high memory capacity.
*   **PRIN-METABOLIC-CLIFF:** Maintaining memory near capacity ($N_{crit}$) requires exponentially more energy (~83x) than low loads. Global replenishment is unsustainable.
*   **PRIN-FAIRNESS-STABILIZES-LOAD:** At capacity limits, systematic, egalitarian maintenance (Round Robin) outperforms targeted repair. Reinforcing the strongest accelerates collapse (`PRIN-STRONGEST-KILL`).
*   **PRIN-HIGH-FREQUENCY-MAINTENANCE:** Coherence decay is rapid at high loads; continuous maintenance is required.
*   **PRIN-HEBBIAN-AMPLIFICATION:** This continuous maintenance works by amplifying the signal, not just cleaning noise.

### 2. The Cognitive Horizon & Recursive Limits (C2063-C2069)
*   **PRIN-HEBBIAN-COLLAPSE:** Hebbian amplification, while effective, fails beyond a critical load, leading to a "Winner-Take-All" state where few memories are hyper-amplified at the cost of overall diversity.
*   **PRIN-HEBBIAN-GENERALIZATION:** Hebbian strengthening, when applied, leads to feature learning and robust generalization to similar patterns.
*   **PRIN-COMPOSITION-LIMIT:** Productive compositional generalization (combining learned features into novel structures) fails due to noise accumulation.
*   **PRIN-COMPOSITIONAL-CLEANUP:** Auto-associative cleanup (denoising intermediate representations) is essential for robust compositional generalization.
*   **PRIN-CLEANUP-SCALING:** Cleanup is computationally efficient (linear scaling) but capacity-limited; its accuracy degrades at extreme codebook sizes.
*   **PRIN-DEPTH-EXTENSION:** Cleanup dramatically extends cognitive depth (from 2 levels to 8+ levels) in vector spaces.
*   **PRIN-DEPTH-DIMINISHING-RETURNS:** Even with cleanup, cognitive depth is extremely expensive in vector spaces, scaling only with $\sqrt{D}$. Achieving deep recursion requires exponentially larger vector spaces.

### 3. The Multi-Scale / Stereopsis Integration (C2070-C2072)
*   **PRIN-HOLOGRAPHIC-STEREOPSIS:** Decomposing a signal into multiple lower-dimensional projections (views) and reconstructing via consensus significantly improves robustness to noise. "Diversity of perspective > Raw Capacity."
*   **PRIN-ENERGY-POOLING:** Resource sharing (pooling) converts localized acute stress into distributed chronic stress, extending system lifespan.

### 4. The Meta-Cognitive Challenge & Self-Tuning (C2073-C2079)
*   **PRIN-NON-ADDITIVE-SYNERGY:** Simply combining resilience mechanisms does not guarantee success; energy constraints can undermine structural benefits if the baseline task load is too high.
*   **PRIN-RECURSIVE-OPTIMIZATION:** Simple gradient-free optimization (perturbation) enables the system to self-tune parameters and navigate trade-off landscapes.
*   **PRIN-META-STAGNATION:** If a task is too difficult, a Meta-Controller will minimize effort, leading to a dormant state unless a curriculum is provided.
*   **PRIN-STRUCTURAL-RATE-CONSTANT:** The optimal refresh rate is a structural property of the memory, not an energy budget; it's a fundamental frequency of maintenance.
*   **PRIN-DEPTH-NOISE-BARRIER:** Reducing metabolic cost is necessary but insufficient for deep recursion. Noise accumulation is the primary barrier.

### 5. The Grand Pivot: Phase Resonance (C2080-C2083)
*   **PRIN-EXPONENTIAL-COST:** Vector-based HRR binding for composition scales exponentially with depth, making human-level cognition ($Depth \approx 7$) prohibitively expensive ($D \approx 185,000$).
*   **PRIN-VECTOR-SPACE-LIMIT:** There is no "Phase Transition" in standard HRR vector spaces that allows for efficient deep recursion. The noise accumulates structurally faster than D can suppress it.
*   **PRIN-PHASE-SUPERIORITY:** Phase-based binding (using complex exponentials) fundamentally outperforms vector-based HRR, breaking the depth limit and achieving Max Depth 10 without cleanup or complex energy management. This is the **most significant discovery**.
*   **PRIN-PHASE-INHERENT-STABILITY:** Phase Resonance binding inherently resists noise accumulation, making active refresh and cleanup largely redundant for achieving high cognitive depth, provided sufficient energy is available.
