### Why Reality-Grounded Systems Are Deterministic

**Three Conditions for Determinism:**

1. **Strong Deterministic Forcing**
   - Energy recharge: 1.2 units/cycle (deterministic, from stable reality metrics)

   - Dominates stochastic perturbations

2. **Bounded State Space**
   - Energy cap: 200 units (physical constraint)
   - Saturation makes noise irrelevant (clamping at boundary)

3. **Short Time to Saturation**
   - t_saturate ≈ 60 cycles (~2% of experiment)
   - Remaining 98% spent at saturated attractor
   - No memory of initial conditions

**Result:** All trajectories converge to same attractor regardless of noise.
