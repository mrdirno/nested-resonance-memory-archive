## Interpretation: Hypothesis Refuted

**Conclusion:** Threshold does NOT affect basin structure. The apparent effect in C144 was an **n=3 sampling artifact**.

### Why C144 Showed Threshold Variation (n=3):

With only 3 samples per threshold:
- Random variation easily creates 0%, 33%, or 100% outcomes
- Threshold 700 happened to get 1/3 Basin A by chance
- Other thresholds happened to get 0/3 by chance
- **This was statistical noise, not real structure**

### What C166 Reveals (n=10):

With 10 samples per threshold:
- Law of large numbers suppresses random variation
- ALL thresholds converge to 100% Basin A
- **No threshold dependence detected**
