## MECHANISM

### Why Sudden Change Fails

**1. No Time for Adjustment**
- Gradual change: Populations track slowly
- Sudden change: No adjustment time
- Mismatch between population and resources

**2. Cascade Collapse**
- Low K → Prey crash
- Prey crash → Predator starvation
- Cascade up the food chain
- Complete extinction

**3. Positive Feedback**
- Lower prey → Higher predation rate per prey
- Accelerates collapse
- No recovery point

### Critical K Threshold

For seven-trophic at standard parameters:
- K ≥ 400: Stable
- K = 200: Immediate collapse
- Critical threshold: ~300
