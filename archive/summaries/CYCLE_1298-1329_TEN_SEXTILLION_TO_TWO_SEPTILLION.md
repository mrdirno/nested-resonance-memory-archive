## KEY FINDINGS

### 1. Perfect Scores (5 unique strategies achieving 100%)
- **0.92× at 2×10^23** - recovered from 80% crash at 5×10^22
- **0.89× at 5×10^23** - moderate-conservative excellence
- **0.86× at 10^24** - most conservative at extreme scale
- **0.89× at 2×10^24** - maintaining leadership
- **0.95× at 2×10^24** - recovered from 85% at 10^24

### 2. Oscillating Resilience Pattern
- No single strategy maintains dominance
- Leadership rotates continuously across scales
- U-shaped recovery patterns (dips followed by recovery)
- Examples:
  - 0.92×: 80% → 100% (recovery across 4 orders of magnitude)
  - 0.95×: 85% → 100% (recovery across 1 order of magnitude)

### 3. Scale Invariance
- System viable from 10^5 (baseline) to 2×10^24
- **19 orders of magnitude** with no fundamental collapse
- Coexistence rates remain 85-100% throughout
- All strategies maintain ≥80% coexistence at all scales

### 4. Strategy Performance Patterns
- **0.86×** (most conservative): Best at 5×10^22 and 10^24
- **0.89×** (moderate-conservative): Most consistent, 3 perfect scores
- **0.92×** (moderate-aggressive): Volatile but recovers
- **0.95×** (aggressive): Best in ties, vulnerable alone
