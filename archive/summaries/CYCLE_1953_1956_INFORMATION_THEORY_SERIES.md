## Information-Theoretic Model

### System Complexity

```
Total Information: H(D,E) = 3.01 bits

Decomposed:
  H(Depth)  = 0.95 bits  (hierarchy structure)
  H(Energy) = 2.34 bits  (energy diversity)
  I(D;E)    = 0.28 bits  (correlation)

Effective States:
  ~1.9 depth levels × ~5.1 energy states ≈ 10 configurations
```

### Information Flow

1. **Births**: Add low-energy agents → increase D0 entropy
2. **Recharge**: Shift energy distribution → moderate change
3. **Composition**: Remove similar pairs → decrease entropy
4. **Decomposition**: Split high-energy → increase lower depth entropy

### Stochastic Dynamics

- Decorrelation time ~110 cycles
- No periodic oscillations
- Fluctuations are noise around attractor
- CV = 7.5% (from C1949)
