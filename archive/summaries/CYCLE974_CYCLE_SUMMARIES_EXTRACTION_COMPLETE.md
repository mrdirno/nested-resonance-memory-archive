   - Rationale: Redundancy = robustness for future discovery

3. **Transparency over optics** (CS-TS-009)
   - Evidence: Bug documentation despite weakness signal

4. **Framework building over isolated findings** (CS-TS-010)
   - Evidence: Multi-scale validation, spawns/agent threshold models
   - Rationale: Reusable methodologies benefit future research

**Significance:** These principles are not abstract aspirations but empirically validated strategies with quantified ROI.
