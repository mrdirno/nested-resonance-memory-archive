## Mechanism Analysis

### Why Optimized System is Resilient

1. **Balanced predation:** Prey populations aren't on the edge of collapse
2. **Energy surplus:** Predators have enough prey to survive temporary shortages
3. **Recovery time:** Populations have buffer cycles to regenerate
4. **No cascade trigger:** Perturbations don't trigger irreversible cascades

### Why Baseline System Was Fragile

At baseline attack (×1.0), populations operated at the edge:
- Any perturbation pushed them into collapse
- No recovery buffer
- L5 was particularly vulnerable due to small population
