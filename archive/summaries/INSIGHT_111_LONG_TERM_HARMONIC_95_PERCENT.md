## THEORETICAL INTERPRETATION

### Long-Term Harmonic Hypothesis

**Definition:** A **long-term harmonic** is a spawning frequency that:
1. Shows baseline behavior at short temporal scales (neutral, seed-driven)
2. Activates at extended temporal scales (>threshold, frequency-driven)
3. Exhibits resonance elevation above baseline (harmonic signature)
4. Requires extended evolution to manifest (latent until threshold)

**95% Frequency Fits All Criteria:**
- ✅ Baseline at 3K-7.5K (dormant phase)
- ✅ Activates at ~10K cycles (sudden onset)
- ✅ Elevates to 67% Basin A (2× baseline - strong resonance)
- ✅ Temporal activation threshold (~8K-10K cycles)

### Mechanism (Speculative)

**Hypothesis:** 95% spawning frequency has a **long-period resonance** with Basin A attractor that requires many cycles to build up coherence.

**Analogy:** Like a resonant cavity that requires many oscillations to reach steady-state amplitude (Q-factor in physics).

**Mechanism:**
1. **Early phase (0-7.5K):** Spawning at 95% introduces periodic perturbations
2. **Coherence build-up (0-10K):** Perturbations gradually phase-align with Basin A oscillations
3. **Threshold crossing (~10K):** Coherence exceeds critical value → resonance activates
4. **Peak expression (10K-15K):** Full constructive interference → maximum basin probability
5. **Partial decay (20K+):** Phase drift, saturation, or higher-order effects reduce coherence

**Testable Prediction:** Phase coherence between spawning events and Basin A transitions should:
- Be random/low at 3K-7.5K cycles
- Sharply increase between 7.5K-10K cycles
- Reach maximum at 10K-15K cycles
- Decrease slightly at 20K+ cycles

### Why Temporal Activation Threshold Exists

**Hypothesis:** Long-period resonances require **time to develop coherent phase alignment**.

**Evidence:**
- 50% harmonic appears immediately at 3K (short-period resonance)
- 95% harmonic appears only at 10K (long-period resonance)
- Ratio: 95/50 = 1.9 (near 2:1 ratio)
- Threshold ratio: 10K/3K ≈ 3.3

**Interpretation:**
- Higher-frequency harmonics (95%) have longer beat periods with Basin A
- Longer beat periods require more cycles to accumulate coherent signal
- Activation threshold scales with resonance period

**Potential Scaling Law:**
```
Activation_Threshold ∝ (Harmonic_Frequency / Base_Frequency)^α
```
where α ≈ 1.5-2 (to be validated with more frequencies)
