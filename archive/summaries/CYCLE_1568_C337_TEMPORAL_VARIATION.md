## MECHANISM

### Why No Population Tracking

**1. Averaging Effect**
- Multiple trophic levels average out variation
- Energy flows smoothed across levels
- Top-down effects dominate bottom-up

**2. Time Constants**
- Population response slower than K oscillation
- Lag prevents tracking
- Equilibrium forms at mean K

**3. Predation Buffering**
- Predators consume excess prey
- Stabilizes prey near carrying capacity
- Food web acts as regulator

### The Buffering Mechanism

```
Environmental Input: K varies 400-800
                          ↓
Level 0 (Prey):     Slight variation (absorbed)
                          ↓
Level 1:            Stabilized (predation)
                          ↓
Levels 2-6:         Constant (cascade damping)
                          ↓
System Output:      Stable populations
```
