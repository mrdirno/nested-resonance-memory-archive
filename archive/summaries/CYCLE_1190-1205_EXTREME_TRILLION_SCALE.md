## KEY FINDINGS

### 1. NO COLLAPSE THRESHOLD AT 100 TRILLION
System maintains majority viability (85-100%) across **1,000× range from 100B to 100T×**. This extends total tested range to **9 orders of magnitude** (10^5 to 10^14).

### 2. TWO PERFECT SCORES
- **0.86× at 20T×**: 100% (20/20) - Conservative strategy peaks
- **0.92× at 50T×**: 100% (20/20) - Moderate strategy peaks

### 3. DRAMATIC U-SHAPED PATTERNS
0.92× shows extreme volatility:
- 75% (10B) → 95% (20B) → 90% (50B-1T) → 85% (10T) → **75%** (20T) → **100%** (50T) → 85% (100T)

0.86× shows sustained recovery:
- 85% (100B-500B) → 95% (1T) → 90% (10T) → **100%** (20T) → 95% (50T-100T)

### 4. CONSERVATIVE DOMINANCE AT EXTREME SCALES
At ultra-extreme scales (10T-100T×), conservative strategies (0.86×, 0.89×) consistently lead:
- 10T×: 0.86×/0.89× tied at 90%
- 20T×: 0.86× leads at 100%
- 100T×: 0.86× leads at 95%

Exception: 50T× where 0.92× achieves perfect 100%

### 5. 0.95× CONSISTENT UNDERPERFORMANCE
At extreme scales, highest attack rate (0.95×) consistently underperforms:
- 10T×: 85%
- 20T×: 85%
- 50T×: 85%
- 100T×: 85%

This suggests aggressive strategies are less robust at ultra-extreme conversion scales.
